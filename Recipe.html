Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl
  3. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden brown
  9. Remove from oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely
  10. Enjoy your delicious cookies!
